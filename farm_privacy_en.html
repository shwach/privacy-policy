Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 30, 2025

Farm Trade ("the App") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information necessary to provide our services.

Advertising Identifier (Ad ID / IDFA) Google AdMob may collect your device's advertising identifier to serve personalized ads and measure ad performance. On iOS 14.5 and later, this is only collected after you grant permission through the App Tracking Transparency (ATT) prompt.
Game Score & Nickname When you use the leaderboard feature, your chosen nickname and game score are stored on our servers.
Automatically Collected Information To improve app stability and performance, we may automatically collect device information (OS version, device model) and crash logs.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To serve advertisements via Google AdMob
  • To operate the leaderboard and ranking system
  • To analyze errors and improve the app

3. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services. Each service's privacy policy applies to the data they collect.

  • Google AdMob — View Privacy Policy
  • Google Firebase (Leaderboard) — View Privacy Policy

4. Data Retention

Collected data is deleted promptly once the purpose of collection has been fulfilled. Leaderboard scores will be deleted upon request.

5. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will promptly delete it.

6. Your Rights

  • Opt out of ad tracking: iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking → turn off tracking for this app
  • Request deletion of leaderboard data: contact us at the email below

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be communicated via an in-app notice or by updating this page.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
