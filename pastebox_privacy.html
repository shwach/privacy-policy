Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

1. Information We Collect

PasteBox does not collect any personal information. All text data you save in the app is stored exclusively on your device or in your own iCloud account — never on our servers.

2. How Your Data Is Stored

  • All clip data is encrypted with AES-256 and stored locally on your device.
  • iCloud Backup (Pro feature) stores data in your personal iCloud account, governed by Apple's security policies.
  • No data is ever transmitted to our servers.

3. Biometric Authentication (Face ID / Touch ID)

The app lock feature uses iOS's LocalAuthentication framework. Biometric data is processed entirely within Apple's Secure Enclave. PasteBox never accesses or stores your biometric information.

4. In-App Purchases

All purchases are handled by the Apple App Store. PasteBox does not collect or store any payment information.

5. Third-Party Services

PasteBox does not use any advertising, analytics, or tracking SDKs. We do not collect or share user behavior data with any third parties.

6. Children's Privacy

PasteBox does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, we will notify you through an app update. Significant changes will be communicated via an in-app notice.
